# Privacy Policy for Wild Chicken University

Welcome to Wild Chicken University! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website. This privacy policy outlines how we handle information associated with your interaction with our website. As a front-end only project, our platform operates without any data collection, storage, or transfer to servers, ensuring a high level of privacy and security.

## Data We Collect

**Personal Data:** Wild Chicken University does not collect any personal data. All interactions are processed locally on your device, and no information is sent or stored externally.

**Usage Data:** No usage data is collected, processed, or stored by Wild Chicken University. All interactions remain within your local device environment.

## Information We Do Not Collect

- **Server Data:** We do not own or operate servers; therefore, no data is uploaded or stored on any external systems.
- **Cookies and Tracking:** Our website does not use cookies or any form of tracking technologies that monitor your activity.

## Data Security

Given that Wild Chicken University operates entirely on the front-end, the security of your data rests within your local device. No data is transmitted over the internet or stored on external servers, thus eliminating the risks associated with data breaches related to server-based storage.

## Data Usage

Since no data is collected, there is no usage of data for any purpose such as marketing, analytics, or third-party sharing. Your interactions with Wild Chicken University remain private and are not analyzed, shared, or used in any way.

## Your Privacy Rights

As Wild Chicken University does not collect or store any personal data, typical privacy rights such as access, correction, erasure, or data portability do not apply. All data is controlled by and remains on your local device, ensuring you full control over your information.

## Children's Privacy

Our website does not specifically target or collect information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no risk of breaching children's privacy.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. However, any changes will be consistent with our front-end only policy, ensuring continued protection of your privacy. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any updates.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please feel free to contact us via email at support@wildchickenuniversity.com.
